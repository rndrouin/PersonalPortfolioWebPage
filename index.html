About Me

My name is Rob Drouin. I am a Manager of Engineering at Liberty Mutual Insurance. I have worked for Liberty Mutual for 15 years in a number of different roles mostly centered around IT.

I grew up in Dover NH and went to the University of New Hampshire. While in college, I played for the UNH tennis program and studied business. I am still an active alumni serving as an alumni advisor for 25 freshmen business students.

After college, I began my career at Liberty Mutual and got my MBA from Bentley University. I have lived in several different towns but have put down roots in Exeter with my wife and two daughters.
